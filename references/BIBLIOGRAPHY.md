## NOTES

### Key gap identified in the literature
No published paper formalizes the complete Dewasa Ayu rule set as a computational
algorithm. Suwintana's fuzzy-logic approach (2014) is the closest but treats rules
as fuzzy membership functions rather than documenting the underlying Wariga rules.
The Wariga BELOG paper (JSI 2022) gives a simpler mod-4 system (GURU/RATU/LARA/PATI).
The compound rules from Wariga Gemet and Wariga Sundari Bungkah remain unpublished
in computational form.

### Unique contribution of this project
No prior work has published a machine-readable extraction of a complete printed
Balinese calendar with all paringkelan fields (watek, lintang, pararasan, gew cycle).
The closest are peradnya's app and Suwintana's kalenderbali.info, neither of which
publishes source data as open datasets. The 2026 OCR corpus (365 days, 30 wukus,
12 sasih, 210 Tri-Pramana entries) fills this gap.
